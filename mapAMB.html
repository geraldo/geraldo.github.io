Mapping expertise and data insights

Map of social innovation

A research about social practices in the area of Barcelona to explore alternative economic models.

This research had a close look at those practices aimed at combat the crisis and to defence rights and promote an alternative economic models.

We collected all experiences, classified them and put them on an easy to use map. In addition to the location of initiatives, you can also activate the display of vulnerable urban areas and affluent urban areas.

The map is collaborative allowing visitors to add new initiatives.
