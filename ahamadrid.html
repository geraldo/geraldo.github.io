Housing Atlas

Online map to visualize housing data.

We have compiled data of more than 16.000 cities and coded it into COG GeoTIFF images. You can compare variations on urbanisation and demography from 1975 till 2015 with absolute data from 2015.

The atlas was put online for for AHA Madrid housing congress in May 2022.

Client: 300.000 Km/s, International Union of Architects
