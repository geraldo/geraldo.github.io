Map of more than 2000 public fruit trees and community gardening projects at Linz/Austria.

The project shows more than 2,000 public fruit trees in the city of Linz. The basic data comes from the Open Data portal of Linz.

The fruits of the trees are freely available common goods, which may be picked and eaten by each person! Furthermore the map shows also community gardens.

Linz pfl端ckt is available as app for Android devices and as a WebApp for Firefox OS. Owners of other phones can use the web version, as it is responsive and thus adapt to your mobile display.
