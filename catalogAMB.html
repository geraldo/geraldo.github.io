Political innovation catalog

A collection of cooperative practices to satisfy social needs in the area of Barcelona.

This research identified politicaly innovative initiatives in the area of Barcelona.

These practices are classified and can be accesed either by municipalities or categories.

Users can sort and filter them according to different criteria like name, town, category, subcategory or keyword.

The catalog is collaborative in the term of allowing visitors to add new initiatives.
