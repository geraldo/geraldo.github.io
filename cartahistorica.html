Historic Charter of Barcelona

26 cartographies explaining the history of the city.

The Historic Charter of Barcelona is a tool to explain the history of the city through the cartographies that describe it. It contains 26 maps that have been drawn from texts, historical cartographies, archaeological maps, modern cartography and contemporary digital maps.

A committee of historians specialized in the various historical eras have cross-checked and corrected the documents under the direction of the Barcelona City History Museum (MUHBA).

Client: 300000 km/s, Barcelona City History Museum (MUHBA)
