City comparison

City board for the Inter-American Development Bank.

Vidor de ciudades is a visualizer to compare key data from 26 cities from Colombia and Mexico.

It offers filters, sorting and grouping for different values and metodologies to make it possible to compare the most important data points from these cities.

Client: IDB, 300000 km/s
