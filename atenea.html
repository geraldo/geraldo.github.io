Recursos Polígono Sur

Map of public, private and commons resources at Polígono Sur/Sevilla.

Marianao Foundation is a non profit organization that intervines with colectives in situation of extrem vulnerability.

We developed a map of public, private and citizens run infrastructure. They are categorized by health, education, social, environment, economy, etc. and can be filtered to limit the shown results to the users interests.
