Geopark Origens Tecnical Planning

Internal tecnical planning map for UNESCO Geopark at the Catalan Pyrenees.

UNESCO Geopark uses this web tool for internal management of the Geopark at the Catalan Pyrenees.

It includes thematic layers like catalogues, conditions for edification and soil destinations combined with the Spanish cadastre.
