Barcelona Itineraries

Mapping 50 years of archivements of the neightbourhood movement.

For the 50 years of the Federation of Neighborhood Associations of Barcelona, itineraries are made through the districts of Barcelona with the complicity of the associations that share and explain the demands for the facilities and spaces achieved by the neighborhoods during these five decades. With the desire to share past and present experiences and with special attention to future challenges.
