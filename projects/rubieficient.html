Portal Rubí Eficient

Interactive map with information about annual consumption of electricity, gas and water of each plot of Rubí.

It is an interactive map that allows you to know, at the level of each plot, the annual consumption of electricity, gas and water, the year of construction of the buildings, among other aspects.

The Portal aims to be a tool that facilitates the comprehensive energy rehabilitation of residential buildings.

All this information helps professionals related to rehabilitation to carry out technical and economic studies to make rehabilitation more agile and based on real data.

On the other hand, it also allows citizens to know the consumption of their neighborhood and whether it increases or decreases over the years.

Client: PSIG, Cityhall Rubí
