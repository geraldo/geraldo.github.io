Risk Factors in highschool students

Highschool survey from Barcelona public health agency.

The public health agency of Barcelona does every four years a survey about essential health questions for highschool students.

The survey FRESC (Risk factors in highschool students) is done since 1987 in representative samples of the educational centers of Barcelona.
