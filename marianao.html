Mapping expertise

Marianao

Map of common goods of Marianao neighbourhood at Sant Boi de Llobregat/Spain.

Marianao Foundation is a non profit organization that develops social and educational projects for the community, with the aim of promoting personal and community development in Sant Boi de Llobregat.

We developed a map of resources that shows local infrastructure ordered by different categories, like health, education, social, environment and economy. A filter allows users to limit the shown results to their interests.
