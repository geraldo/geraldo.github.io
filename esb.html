Barcelona Health Survey

Visualizing results of reports from Barcelona public health agency.

The public health agency of Barcelona does every five years a survey about essential health questions.

This tool reads the raw data of the survey and visualises the most important aspects as charts and maps. Additionally all raw data can be accessed and exported to different formats.

The motor behind this project is able to import different editions of the survey. This makes it possible to add past and future editions of the survey.
