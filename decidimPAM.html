Decidim PAM Barcleona

Visualizing the participatory process of the municipal action plan.

As part of the changes coming with the new mayor in 2015, Barcelona launched a participatory web site to allow citizens take part in preposals and decisions.

The first participatiry process on Decidim Barcelona was about the municipal action plan. More than 20.000 people sent more than 10.000 proposals and made more than 200.000 digital interactions.

This project resumes the process and visualizes the numbers behind the process.

Client: Barcelona City Council
